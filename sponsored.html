Sponsored (VIP) Mirrors

You can help pay for the cost of a server for use with FTP or rclone-based tools (Rookie, RcloneBrowser/Quest Archive GUI, Sidenoder).

REMINDER: Payment is NEVER required to access the Free Quest Games Mirror.

Cost & Requirements

Cost: $1 USD worth of crypto (BCH preferred) for a month. You must use Discord to DM us. We only accept the Cryptocurrencies listed on the Quest Archive Wallets page.

How to get started

  1. Send $1 to $6 USD worth to our Wallets based on duration.
  2. Send a DM via Discord to our new Quest Archive-Bot with the command !verify.
  3. Check your DMs for instructions and the configuration file.
