How This Works

The group was founded by members who are no longer active. Currently, the team consists of volunteers who manage the operations during their free time.

The Team

  • Maxine: Admin, jack of all trades
  • JarJarBlinkz: Lead tester, guru of the APK
  • Moderators: Gato, Kenny, waterchip, aemee, Encarnação, Appolonius

How Games Get Updated

We primarily rely on community donations. When you launch Rookie, it scans for games that need an update and prompts you to upload them.

Testing

We test every game before release by sideloading each onto a headset and evaluating functionality.

Why Sponsored Mirrors?

Running this operation incurs costs for servers and bandwidth. Most of our funding comes from the sponsored mirror to keep services free for most users.
