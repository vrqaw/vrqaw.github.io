Android Downloading Guide

Sideloading from Android is complex but possible with Bugjaeger. Note: Free Mirrors do not work with this approach.

Step 1: Connecting

Launch Bugjaeger and connect your phone to your Quest via cable. Accept all prompts.

Step 2: Installing APK

Click the plus icon in Bugjaeger and select "Choose APK file". Browse and select your APK.

Step 3: Copying OBB

Create a folder in sdcard -> Android -> obb with the same package name and upload the OBB files.
