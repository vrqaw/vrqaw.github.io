Sidenoder Guide

Sidenoder is an alternative sideloader for various platforms.

How to Use

Simply make sure your device is connected by checking for a DEVICE | connected status. Drag and drop your APK file into Sidenoder.

Sponsored Mirrors

Change your "Path Config" to your config file in Settings to make Sidenoder work with our mirrors.
