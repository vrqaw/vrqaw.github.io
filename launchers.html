Alternative Launchers

Instead of "Unknown Sources", you can use custom launchers for your Quest.

Available Launchers

  • Lightning Launcher: Focused on speed, flexibility, and convenience. Supports both official and sideloaded apps.
  • Evolve Launcher: 2D interface for your VR experience.
  • Quest App Launcher: Implemented in Unity.
  • Quest Games Optimizer (QGO): Paid tool for graphics tweaking and performance.
